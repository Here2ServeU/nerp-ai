# NERP Overview

NERP stands for Naweji Enterprise Reliability Platform. In this beginner project, NERP is introduced as a simple operational intelligence loop:

1. Collect telemetry from systems.
2. Analyze telemetry with AI and ML models.
3. Convert model output into decisions.
4. Execute a remediation or monitoring action.

The goal is not to build a production platform immediately. The goal is to understand how data, models, logic, and automation fit together.

## Why NERP Matters

- Modern systems generate large amounts of signals.
- Operators need fast and consistent responses.
- AI can help prioritize risk before incidents become outages.
- Automation reduces manual operational work.

## The Beginner Mental Model

Think of NERP as four connected layers:

- Data layer: collects metrics and records.
- AI layer: predicts or detects unusual conditions.
- Decision layer: maps outputs to actions.
- Automation layer: executes safe responses.

This repository keeps each layer small and readable so you can learn the architecture without unnecessary complexity.
